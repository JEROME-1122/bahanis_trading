About US

Bahanis Trading Company LLC with its passion for cutting edge technology, strong market commitments and business principles, is ideally positioned to become one of the reputed names in the Building & Construction Materials Trading sector in the Sultanate of Oman. We are Equipped with sufficient stock of materials and professional staff, who would offer excellent backup and prompt customer service and assure our customers value added service with superior quality products.

Our wide range of products are manufactured and quality tested in accordance with the international standards such as BS, BSEN, DIN, ASTM and ISO. In very short span of time, we have evolved to be a Major Reliable Supplier to most of the Consumers and Contractors in the market.

Our Registered Brands: Majan, Hoben, Seal, Armstrong, BTC, Haima, Diamond, Athletico.

Agency Products: Atlantic, Dutron, Duplon, Hydrofit, Flexitub, Weldall, Thumb, Atlantic welding rods, Eason, Berent, Atlas, S+H.

Marketed Products: Peta, Sumoflex, Prime trap, Ecco, Simbakit, ERA, Tramontina, Tombo, Golden bridge, Workmaster, Indica, Sykper, Uken, Stanley, Tagit, Uniflo.

Bahanis Trading Company LLC, your one-stop destination for premium building materials and sanitary ware, tailored to your project needs. Dedicated to providing exceptional solutions and support for all your construction requirements.
